Explore Tokyo

Tokyo, the capital of Japan, is a dazzling metropolis blending ultra-modern skyscrapers, technology, and vibrant pop culture with traditional temples, shrines, and gardens. It’s a city of contrasts and endless exploration.

Top Attractions


  • Tokyo Tower
  • Senso-ji Temple
  • Shibuya Crossing
  • Meiji Shrine
  • Tokyo Skytree

Suggested Activities


  • Shopping in Ginza and Harajuku
  • Explore Akihabara for anime and electronics
  • Enjoy sushi at Tsukiji Market
  • Visit traditional tea houses and gardens
